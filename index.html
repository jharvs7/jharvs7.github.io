Social Media's Grasp on the Movie Industry

When was the last time you watched a movie without first hearing about it on social media? Social media has completely taken over American life. The movie industry is no exception. It changed the way movies are made by breaking down barriers between the audience and actors while also giving marketing power back into the hands of the movie studios. In our first podcast "The Grasp of Social Media on the Movie Industry" the hosts of Luxrays The Podcast: Kyleigh Dawson, Sarai Alba, Jackson Lee, Al Kang, Jake Harville, and Prahaara Malli Sh will discuss their thoughts on this phenomenon.
